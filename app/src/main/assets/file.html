1. INTRODUCTION

  • 1.1 P.S. Group Reality Private Limited (PS Group) is one of the leading developers engaged in Real Estate.
  • 1.2 For the purpose of rewarding its customers PS Group has conceived and/or formulated a Scheme to be known as “HAPPY OURS’’ (referred to as “the Scheme”) and PS Group through its marketing agent namely 380 Realty Solution LLP having its office at 1002, EM Bypass, Kolkata-700105 is implementing the Scheme. In terms of the said Scheme any customer of PS Group or its associate companies or its associate entities (hereinafter collectively referred to as “PS Group”) introduces a person to acquire a Unit in selected PS Group projects, or self-purchase of another unit from PS Group will be rewarded in the manner as hereinafter appearing. Such persons rewarded with points is referred to as “the Recipient”. A Recipient is eligible to earn reward points on the basis of:
    • a. The Recipient being a customer of PS Group. A customer is an individual/company/any legal entity who has purchased a property with PS Group.
    • b. Successful direct referrals shared with PS Group, which do not have any other source for closure of such sale. Such referrals would remain valid for a period of 60 days from the date the lead is shared. Referral is valid and considered applicable only when the referral has signed the agreement of sale. After execution of the agreement of sale, the person referring shall be a recipient and will be eligible for the reward point as per the scheme. May it be clarified that in the event of cancellation of such Agreement for Sale the recipient shall be entitled to such reward points.
    • c. In the said scheme a recipient will be eligible to redeem the reward points only if the recipient has signed an agreement of sale with PS Group for purchase of a select PS Group property.
    • d. In the said Scheme any new customer shall be entitled to welcome reward points if he/she has bought the property through a referral and has signed the agreement of sale.
    • e. This program is applicable to selected PS Group projects. The project where this scheme is applicable shall be shared on request from time to time from PS Group.
  • The aforesaid shall be formulated in the manner as hereinafter appearing.

2. SCHEME

  • 2.1 SILVER MEMBERSHIP: In terms of the Scheme formulated by PS Group to be known as HAPPY OURS a customer shall be entitled to ‘reward points’ as hereinafter appearing.
    • a. Referral: If a customer introduces any new flat/property buyer for acquiring a Unit in selected projects of PS Group shall be jointly entitled to ‘reward points’ as hereinafter appearing:
      • i. A new customer (referee) on reference of an existing customer (shall mean a person, company or any legal entity who has purchased a property/properties with PS Group worth Rs. 1 Cr. or less, hereinafter referred to as the “Referrer”) purchases a unit in any of the selected PS Group project applicable under the scheme, then the points accumulated shall be 1% of the purchase value (purchase value is total consideration, including parking, excluding taxes and extra charges). The said accumulated points shall be distributed between the existing customer (referrer) and the new customer (referee), wherein the Existing Customer shall be entitled to 75% of the said accumulated points and New Customer shall be entitled to 25% of the said accumulated points.
      • ii. As per the scheme if a Referrer gives a cumulative reference sales of value more than INR 6 crores in a financial year then in the subsequent future reference the Referrer shall be entitled to reward points earn to the tune of 1.2% of the purchase value (purchase value is total consideration, including parking, excluding taxes and extra charges). The said accumulated points accrued by such subsequent reference shall be distributed between the existing customer (referrer) and the new customer (referee), wherein the Existing Customer shall be entitled to 75% of the said accumulated points and New Customer shall be entitled to 25% of the said accumulated points.
      • iii. As per the scheme if a Referrer gives a cumulative reference sales value of more than INR 15 crores in a financial then in the subsequent future reference the reward points earn rate shall be 1.5% of the purchase value (purchase value is total consideration, including parking, excluding taxes and extra charges). The said accumulated points accrued by such subsequent reference shall be distributed between the existing customer (referrer) and the new customer (referee), wherein the Existing Customer shall be entitled to 75% of the said accumulated points and New Customer shall be entitled to 25% of the said accumulated points.
    • b. Repurchase: A customer who repurchases any new unit/property in selected projects of PS Group shall be entitled to ‘reward points’ as hereinafter appearing:
      • i. If a customer (who has purchased a property/properties with PS Group worth Rs. 1 Cr. or less) purchases another PS property of any value in any of the selected PS Group project applicable under the scheme, then the customer will be entitled to reward points to the value of 1% of the purchase value (purchase value is total consideration, including parking, excluding taxes and extra charges).
      • ii. If a customer repurchases a selected PS property having a cumulative consideration (of all properties) which ranges more than Rs. 1 crores and less than Rs. 3 Crores, then in subsequent future reference or repurchase the reward points earn rate shall be 1.2% of the purchase value (purchase value is total consideration, including parking, excluding taxes and extra charges).
      • iii. If a customer repurchases a selected PS property having a cumulative consideration (of all properties) which ranges more than Rs. 3 Crores, then in subsequent future reference or repurchase the reward points earn rate shall be 1.5% of the purchase value (purchase value is total consideration, including parking, excluding taxes and extra charges).

    • 2A. CLOSURE OF REFERENCE
      • i. As laid down hereinabove a reference may be closed on occurrence of any of the following events:
        • a. If the new customer/ Referee has signed an agreement of sale with PS Group for purchase of a select PS Group property;
        • Or,
        • b. If the Referee has declined the offer.
        • (Any of the events mentioned hereinabove shall mean and be referred as “Closure of Existing Referrals”)
      • ii. It is understood that a customer can make a maximum of 10 references and shall not be entitled to provide further referrals until and unless there has been closure of existing referrals. In other words a customer may have maximum of 10 unclosed referrals at a time. As and when the existing referrals are closed the customer will be able to make new references.
    • 3. REWARD POINTS
      • 3.1 Each Reward point will be of a face value of Rs. 1000. All reward points will be calculated based on the total consideration, including parking, excluding taxes and extra charges payable under the Sale Agreement.
      • 3.2 The reward points shall be credited to the Recipients loyalty account as and when the Sale Agreement of the referred customer is executed and registered.
      • 3.3 The reward points must be redeemed within 24 months of its accrual. The reward points would lapse if not redeemed within 24 months from the date such points have accrued in the Recipients loyalty account.
    • 4. REDEMPTION OF POINTS
      • 4.1 A Recipient may redeem the reward points by Redeeming Points in cash only if the recipient has signed an agreement of sale with PS Group.
      • 4.2 Process of Redemption: Send redemption request to 380 Realty Solution LLP being the marketing agent of PS Group by stating how many points you want to redeem and NEFT transfer details. Based on the request, the amount will be transferred or cheque mailed to the requested address within committed date from the date of receipt of request. 380 Realty Solution LLP shall be liable to make all payments
      • 4.3 Redemption of loyalty points are subject to applicable taxes as per law and the Recipient shall hereby agrees and acknowledges to sign and/provide necessary documents for redemption of points in cash
